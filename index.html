Eid Mubarak, My Dearest Bestie Ushna 🌙🤍
On this beautiful and blessed occasion of Eid-ul-Fitr, I want to take a moment to thank Allah for blessing my life with someone as precious as you.

Ushna, your presence in my life means more than words can ever express. You are not just my best friend you are a beautiful part of my journey, someone who brings comfort, happiness, and light into my days.

Your kindness, your heart, and the way you care for others make you truly special. I feel incredibly grateful to have a bestie like you who understands me, supports me, and stands by me through every moment. Life feels easier and brighter because you are in it. 🌸

On this blessed Eid, I pray that Allah fills your life with endless happiness, peace, and success. May your heart always remain as pure and beautiful as it is, and may all your dreams come true. May Allah bless you with health, prosperity, and countless reasons to smile not just today, but every single day. 🤲

Thank you for being such a precious part of my life. Your friendship is a gift that I will always cherish. I pray that our bond only grows stronger with time and that we continue to create beautiful memories together. 💕

May this Eid bring you joy, love, and countless blessings. Eid Mubarak, my precious bestie Ushna. You truly mean the world to me. 🤍

Sacred Duas For You 🤲
Opening Blessing تَقَبَّلَ اللّٰهُ مِنَّا وَمِنْكُمْ Taqabbal Allahu minna wa minkum May Allah accept (good deeds) from us and from you 🌙
Dua for Happiness & Well-being اَللّٰهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ الْعَفْوَ وَالْعَافِيَةَ فِي الدُّنْيَا وَالآخِرَةِ Allahumma inni as'alukal-afwa wal-afiyata fid-dunya wal-akhirah O Allah, I ask You for pardon and well-being in this world and in the Hereafter 💕
Dua for This World & Hereafter رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِي الآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ Rabbana atina fid-dunya hasanatan wa fil-akhirati hasanatan wa qina 'adhaban-nar Our Lord, give us good in this world and good in the Hereafter, and protect us from the punishment of the Fire ✨
Dua for a Blessed Life اَللّٰهُمَّ بَارِكْ لَنَا فِيمَا رَزَقْتَنَا وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ Allahumma barik lana fima razaqtana wa qina 'adhaban-nar O Allah, bless us in what You have provided for us and protect us from the punishment of the Fire 🌸
Eid Mubarak Greeting Dua كُلَّ عَامٍ وَأَنْتِ بِخَيْرٍ Kulla 'amin wa anti bikhayr May you be well every year Eid Mubarak 🌙

"May Allah accept our fasts, our prayers, and our good deeds. May this Eid be the most beautiful one yet for you, Ushna full of love, laughter, and all things lovely." 🌙✨
